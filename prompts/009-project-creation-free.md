Create a public Free-tier product repository for one of these projects: `bashrc-manager`, `screenshot-worklog`, `personal-ops-manager`, or `local-file-converter`.

The output should include a public-safe README, product spec, minimal source structure, tests or smoke checks, and a GitHub-ready workflow. Keep the scope local-first and open-core. Do not include sync services, paid automations, AI governance, customer data handling, private integrations, or proprietary implementation details.
